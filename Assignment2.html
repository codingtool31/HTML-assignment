Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are my favourit food . here is the receipy of this dish.

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a meduim heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs fron the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat.
